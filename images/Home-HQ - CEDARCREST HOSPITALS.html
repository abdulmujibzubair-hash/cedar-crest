A Major Leap in Cancer Care for Abuja.

The United States Trade Development Agency (USTDA) has awarded a crucial healthcare infrastructure grant to Cedarcrest Hospitals, marking a pivotal moment in Nigeria's fight against cancer. This grant will fund a state-of-the-art cancer treatment center in Abuja, providing vital diagnosis and treatment to 1,000 patients annually. This collaboration brings cutting-edge medical technology and expertise to Nigeria, enhancing healthcare infrastructure and improving lives.
